XK300 Specifications

Built for senior living.

The Xandar Kardian XK300 uses ultra-wideband radar technology to detect micro-movements of the chest wall from up to 8 meters away. Mounted on a wall or ceiling, it monitors an entire room without cameras, wearables, or any patient interaction.
